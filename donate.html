Living with Scleroderma

My Story — Hi, I’m Ohiorenua.

I live with scleroderma, a rare autoimmune condition that hardens the skin and can affect internal organs. Every day involves managing pain, taking medications, and covering ongoing medical costs — all while trying to keep hope and normalcy.

This journey isn't easy, but I choose to keep going: to share my story, raise awareness, and remind anyone facing illness that they are not alone.

Your support helps me afford treatment and stay stable.
